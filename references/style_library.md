# 绘画风格库

## 1. 日系动漫风格 (Anime Style)
- **核心描述**: Japanese anime style, vibrant and cel-shaded colors, clean and sharp line art, expressive characters.
- **进阶描述**: Visual Novel Cover style, cinematic ensemble composition, high-angle wide shot, intense atmosphere.
- **参考风格**: Studio Ghibli, Makoto Shinkai, Ufotable.

## 2. 电影感风格 (Cinematic Style)
- **核心描述**: Cinematic shot, dramatic lighting, epic composition, wide-angle lens (35mm), shallow depth of field.
- **色彩建议**: Moody tones, vibrant colors, desaturated look.

## 3. 赛博朋克 (Cyberpunk Style)
- **核心描述**: Futuristic cityscape, neon lights, holographic advertisements, rainy atmosphere, electric blues and vibrant pinks.

## 4. 艺术绘画类
- **水彩 (Watercolor)**: Soft and translucent colors, visible brush strokes, wet-on-wet technique.
- **油画 (Oil Painting)**: Van Gogh style, thick impasto brushstrokes, swirling lines, vibrant emotional palette.

## 5. 特殊排版风格 (Typography & Layout)
- **地面设计**: White brick pavement covered with oversized black English Typography (e.g., JUMP / HEROES / LEGENDS / ANIME UNIVERSE).
- **构图**: High-angle bird's eye view, characters arranged in a loose but dramatically tense way, clear foreground and background depth.
